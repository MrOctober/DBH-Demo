How to Use Application

Welcome to the DC Department of Behavioral Health’s Online Resource Guide. This application was designed for use on your mobile device. Use it to locate service providers in your neighborhood. Please click on ‘Filter by Ward’, ‘Filter by Location’ or ‘Filter by Services’ to locate behavioral health and other related service providers around the District. Use the map feature below to find the closest Metro station. After you find the services you need, click on the marker icon for details about each service location. If you know of a community-based service provider you think should be included here, please email the site administrator at southcapitol.programs@dc.gov. Thank you for visiting.

Call Access HelpLine

Call the Access HelpLine at 1.888.7WE.HELP or 1.888.793.4357.
